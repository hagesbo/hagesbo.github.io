Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025.8.14 (Year, Month, Day)

This Privacy Policy explains how Hagesbo (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information. Our game only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game Progress Data: Virtual balance (coins, points, etc.), unlocked skins, selected skin, and gameplay settings such as inverted controls.
  • Age Confirmation: A simple “Yes/No” confirmation to check if you are over 18. We do not store your exact age or any other personal details.

Information Third-Party-Services Might Collect

  • Google Play Services (Leaderboard & Achievements): We use Google Play Games Services to enable features such as leaderboards and achievements. This may require access to your Google account data such as your player ID and associated achievements.
  • Google Pay (In-App Purchases): If you make any in-app purchases using Google Pay, certain transaction data (such as purchase details) may be collected and stored by Google for the purpose of processing payments.
  • AdMob (Advertisements): We use AdMob to display ads in our game. AdMob may collect anonymous data such as device information, advertising identifiers, and interactions with ads. This is used to serve personalized advertisements and measure ad performance.

How We Use Your Information

The information we store is used solely to save your in-game progress, apply your chosen gameplay settings, and ensure compliance with age-related restrictions. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access or loss of your game data. No personal information is transmitted to us or to external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
